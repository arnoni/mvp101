Privacy Policy

DillDrill is a privacy-first tool. We do not require accounts for free usage.

Data We Collect:

  • Anonymous Device ID (stored in a cookie for quota management).
  • IP Address (used for rate limiting and abuse prevention).
  • Coordinates (processed temporarily to find results, never stored).

Cookies:

  • dd_anon_id: Tracks your device quota.
  • dd_paid_session: Manages paid access (if applicable).
  • last_result_ids: Enables KMZ download continuity.

No personal data is sold or shared.
